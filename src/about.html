Where I am now.

Hello, I am Christopher Baker, a web developer student at Coder Academy's intensive program in Brisbane Queensland. I joined this program in order to further develop my software technical abilities, skills and I enjoy the challenge of an intensive program. Prior to Coder Academy, I was studying Computer Science at the University of Queensland in Saint Lucia. Outside of software, I enjoy playing lacrosse, video games and helping out my community. Currently, I sit as the President of the Queensland Lacrosse Association, we oversee all of the Lacrosse development happening within Queensland, and I am a volunteer rural firefighter with the Bellthorpe Rural Fire Brigade.

May 2013 - Graduated Fire Academy

In 2013, I graduated from the Santa Rosa Junior College Fire Academy in Santa Rosa California. This was an immense accomplishment in my life because this academy required me to be training from 8am to 5pm everyday, pass daily exams, fitness tests and follow strict protocols. I was also concurrently enrolled into an arithmia course because I wanted to become a firefighter-paramedic. What made this such an achievement in my life was the ability to challenge myself on a daily basis in order to pass these hurdles while maintaining my job. I graduated the academy with distinction as a Squad Leader.
